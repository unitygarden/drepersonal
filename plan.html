WHO WE ARE

Unity Garden is a non-profit that raises financing and charitable donations to purchase land and property, transforming them into thriving, fruitful gardens, invigorating communities and transforming the lives of those with inadequate access to nutritious, healthy food.

WHAT WE DO

Unity Garden's programs and activities include investing in micro-farming and urban horticulture social enterprises that deliver affordable, highly nutritious foods and services to low-income communities in developing neighborhoods throughout the U.S. and the world; spreading the organization's approach through proactive outreach through the use of rich media, communications and knowledge-sharing; and providing leadership developmental training, particularly urban horticulture.
